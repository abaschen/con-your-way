Welcome to Con Your Way!

A strategic duel of cellular automata

How to Play

  1. Setup Phase: Each player places up to 20 cells on their half of the board
  2. Program: Choose 5 instructions that all your cells will execute
  3. Battle: Watch your cells execute their programs while Conway's Game of Life rules apply

Instructions

  • MOVE: Move forward in facing direction
  • TURN_LEFT: Rotate 90° counter-clockwise
  • TURN_RIGHT: Rotate 90° clockwise
  • REPRODUCE: Spawn a child cell ahead (if empty)
  • KILL: Remove any cell directly ahead
  • IDLE: Do nothing

Conway's Rules

After each instruction cycle, Conway's Game of Life rules apply:

  • Cells with 2-3 neighbors survive
  • Cells with <2 or >3 neighbors die
  • Empty cells with exactly 3 neighbors become alive

Victory Conditions

  • Elimination: Opponent has no cells remaining
  • Stalemate: Board unchanged for 3 ticks (winner by cell count)
  • Timeout: 500 ticks elapse (winner by cell count)
💡 Tip: Right-click cells during setup to change their direction!
